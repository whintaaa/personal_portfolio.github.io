About Me

Hello! I'm Whinta Virginia Putri, a fresh graduate in Informatics Engineering who is passionate about data analysis, web development, and design. I enjoy combining technical skills and creativity to create innovative, functional, and user-friendly digital solutions.

Throughout my journey, I have built various projects such as machine learning prediction apps, web applications with PHP & Laravel, and UI/UX prototypes with Figma. I also actively contribute as a graphic designer and event organizer in tech communities.

When I’m not coding or designing, I love exploring new tech tools, keeping up with design trends, and collaborating in communities that foster innovation and creativity.

My approach is detail-oriented, collaborative, and adaptive. Whether it’s data projects, web development, or creative design, I strive to deliver impactful results that inspire and connect people.
